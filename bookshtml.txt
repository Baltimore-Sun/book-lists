Social media platforms have more power over expression than any government in history. Traditional journalism struggles with new economic models while facing attacks on its credibility. Academic institutions wrestle with balancing free inquiry against calls for trigger warnings and safer spaces.</p><p style='margin-bottom:16px;'>These aren’t just policy debates—they’re questions that affect your daily life.
